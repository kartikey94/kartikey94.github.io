Concept Art

It's a form of illustration used to convey an idea that uses in or based upon films, video games, animation, comic books or other media.

Concept Art is mix form of VFX and Design. It always had to cover many subjects, being the primary medium in film poster design since the early days of Hollywood, but the two most widely-covered themes are science fiction and fantasy.
